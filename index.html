Meet the TBay Bros Team

TBay Bros is a team of skilled handymen with over 15 years of combined experience in home maintenance, carpentry, and small construction projects. We started as a small family business and have grown through word-of-mouth and our reputation for quality work.

Our mission is simple: provide reliable, high-quality handyman services with a personal touch. We take pride in our work and treat every home like it's our own.

From fixing a leaky faucet to installing a garden cabin, no job is too small or too big for our team. We're fully insured, licensed, and committed to exceeding your expectations.
